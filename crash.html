CrashhCrazy

CrashhCrazy is a 21 year old producer from Atlanta, Georgia. Crashh is super versatile with the capability to make an instrumental for almost any genre. He finds inspiration in other producers such as wheezy, timbaland, and cardogotwings. Crashh makes music because it provides him a safe haven where he can express himself and clear his mind from the stresses of life.
