Encarnarcion

Encarnarcion is a 20 year old alternative/screamo rap artist from Tampa, Florida who draws influence from artists such as XXXTentacion, $uicideBoy$, Pouya, Skimask the Slumpgod and Mac Miller. Encarn uses his music to connect with other like minded individuals while also speaking on larger issues he sees within society with lyrical, often times screamed, bars over hard-hitting production.
