GIE

GIE is a 22 year old Alt/Pop artist from Stone Mountain, Georgia. GIE draws influences from artists such as Prince, The Weeknd, Frank Ocean, and ekkstacy. GIE makes music because of his love for art and the escape from reality it gives him and the opportunity to connect with others from across the world through that art.
