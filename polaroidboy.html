PolaroidBoy

PolaroidBoy is a 20 year old emo alternative/pop punk artist from Toronto, Canada whose sound has been influenced by the likes of Kurt Cobain and Lil Peep. To PolaroidBoy music is not only therapy, but his entire life. PolaroidBoy hopes to use his art to connect with others who have nothing but music keeping them going. PolaroidBoy stands with those who society has labeled outcasts, misfits, and losers and hopes to bring them a sense of belonging and healing through his art and hopefully make the pain they feel on a daily basis a little more bearable.
